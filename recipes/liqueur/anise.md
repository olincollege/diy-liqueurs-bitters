# Croatian Anise Liqueur

## Ingredients

* 40 g fresh anise, chopped **or** 15 g anise seed and star anise
* 1 stick cinnamon
* 250 g sugar
* 1 l base spirit (40%)

## Directions

1. Place chopped anise, cinnamon, sugar, and base spirit into jar.
2. Let sit in a dark place for 6 weeks, shaking occasionally.
3. Filter and bottle.
